A GLANCE TO KERALA'S BEAUTY

Kerala is well known as God’s Own Country around the world. Kerala owing places where you can go in any season of the year. Along with Natural wonders like beaches, mountains, waterfalls, and rivers, Kerala is known for its culture, temples, festivals, and traditional architecture. These are the things which make Kerala a a famous holiday destination. Tourist Places in Kollam are just worked as icing on the cake. The beautiful coastal city of Kollam or Quilon and Desinganadu (as formerly known) sits on the banks of the serene Lake of Ashtamudi. Kollam city holds the prestige of being one of the oldest ports and trading centers for ages. The essence of its decade long commercial expertise still exists as the exquisite trading skills of the present city. Kerala is such a beautiful tourist destination in India. It considered one of the paradises in the world. Kerala never fails to satisfy its tourists who come to visit here. Kerala is famous for its backwaters, beaches, natural wonders, mesmerizing resorts, and its traditional architecture. These are the things that attract people throughout the year like Wayand, Munnar, Thrissur and many more. Here we will see one of those amazing cities of Kerala called “Cultural Capital of Kerala” – Thrissur. Kerala has earned the reputation of the most popular tourist destination owing to the beauty of the state. It has multiple landscapes like beaches, backwaters, the flora and fauna, the beautiful hills and valleys with tea and spice plantations. Besides, commercial cities also offer many tourist destinations to cherish. There are many experiences that can only be savored in Kerala like the houseboats on the backwaters. Apart from Munnar, Alleppey, Kochi, Thekkady and Trivandrum, one more place is most amazing to visit in Kerala and That is Wayanad. Kerala is blessed enough with natural beauty and Wayanad tourist places add some extra ease in its beauty. Do you know that Kerala is just not about the Hill Stations, Backwaters, Houseboat or Wildlife Sanctuaries. Kerala has much more to offer that’s the reason Kerala is one of the best tourist destination of India. Kerala has 40+ Dams and Reservoirs and many offers good views for tourists. Yes, tourists are allowed to visit some dams in Kerala. So, let us help you to identify famous dams of Kerala. Once in a lifetime visiting Kerala is on everybody’s bucket list. From weather to tradition, art forms to spices, adventurous sports to peaceful Yoga-Meditation, from food to drinks Kerala is a complete package for holidays. You can plan with either family or friends, in January or December, after the monsoon or before summers; Kerala will never disappoint you. Though, every planned trip to Kerala would be insufficient in comparison to the number of things to do it has.
